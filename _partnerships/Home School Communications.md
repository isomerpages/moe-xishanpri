<p><strong>Our Service Standards</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Service Standards for Response Times</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The school commits to the following response timeframes:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>3 working days:</strong> Acknowledge all feedback and reply to
straightforward enquiries.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>7 working days:</strong> For cases needing investigation, including
gathering statements and reviewing evidence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>21 working days:</strong> For complex cases involving detailed
investigations, multiple parties, or external agencies.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>In all cases, the reporting party will be kept informed of progress and
any delays that may occur due to exceptional circumstances.</p>
</li>
</ol>
