<p>At Xishan Primary School, the Sports Club encourages every child to discover
the joy of sports and develop a lifelong love for an active and healthy
lifestyle.</p>
<p>The Sports Club CCA offers a modular sports programme providing the students
with the opportunity to experience at least three different sports each
year. Through a variety of fun and engaging activities, students develop
fundamental movement skills while building confidence across a range of
sports. As they progress through the years, they revisit different sports
to deepen their skills and game concepts.</p>
<p>Beyond learning sports skills, students develop important life skills
such as teamwork, resilience, discipline and leadership. Sports Club members
also serve as GrowWell Champions, leading by example to promote active
living and healthy habits within the school community. They support school-wide
wellness initiatives and inspire their peers to make healthy choices every
day.</p>
