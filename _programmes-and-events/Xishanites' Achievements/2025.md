<p><strong>ArithmetEQ Challenge</strong> is a national math competition Hosted
by Yishun Secondary School. It aims to strengthen students' understanding
of arithmetic operations and equations using the ArithmetEQ game that is
inspired by Scrabble.</p>
<p>In <strong>2025</strong>, Two of our students, <strong>Ma Chui Ling</strong> and <strong>Kaitlyn Yeo Xin Shi</strong>,
managed to clinch a place among the <strong>top 6</strong> pairs of the challenge.</p>
