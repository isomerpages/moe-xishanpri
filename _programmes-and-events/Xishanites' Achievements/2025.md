<h4><strong>Mother Tongue department Achievements</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Chinese Language</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<p><strong>National Chinese Challenge</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Zhang Yihui (6 Care) </strong>achieved the <strong>Best Individual Award</strong> in
the <strong>National Chinese Challenge</strong>. It is an annual event organised
by <strong>SPH Media Trust’s Chinese Media Group</strong> and <strong>Nanyang Girls’ High School</strong>.
The competition brings together top young Chinese language learners from
primary and secondary schools across Singapore, challenging them in areas
such as Mandarin proficiency, comprehension, and effective communication</p>

<p><strong>Chinese Text Recital Competition</strong>
</p>
<p>Our students took part in the Annual Chinese Text Recital Competition,
and we are proud to announce that Chen Boya (3 Respect), Chuah Qin En (3
Resilience), and Agnes Liu Xu Tong (6 Care) received Bronze Awards in the
Primary School Individual Category. Isabella Chin (3 Respect), Feng Mingchen

Bronze Awards in the Primary School Group Category.</p>
<p><strong>“SCRIPT IT RIGHT” Chinese Radio Drama Competition</strong>
</p>
<p>Zhang Yihui (6 Care) was awarded the Merit Award under the Primary School
category in the "SCRIPT IT RIGHT" Chinese Radio Drama Competition 2025.
This competition was jointly organised by the Committee to Promote Chinese
Language Learning (CPCLL), UFM100.3, and Holy Innocents' High School. In
a field of 149 primary school participants from 28 schools, Zhang Yihui's
entry, "六十味" ("The Sixty Flavours"), stood out as one of the five scripts
in Category A to be honoured with a Merit Award.</p>
