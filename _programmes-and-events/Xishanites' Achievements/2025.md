<h4>Yishun Secondary School ArithmetEQ Challenge</h4>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>ArithmetEQ Challenge</strong> is a national math competition Hosted
by Yishun Secondary School. It aims to strengthen students' understanding
of arithmetic operations and equations using the ArithmetEQ game that is
inspired by Scrabble.</p>
<p>In 2025, Two of our students, <strong>Ma Chui Ling</strong> and <strong>Kaitlyn Yeo Xin Shi</strong>,
managed to clinch a place among the top 6 pairs of the challenge.</p>
<p>Other participants: <strong>Joshua Lee Jue Xu, Chew Yong Yi Manfred, Tan Xin Yi Eva, Ng Qi Zhi Zephyr, Adam Julius Lim Yoon Cheng, Elizabeth Choo Ziwei</strong>
