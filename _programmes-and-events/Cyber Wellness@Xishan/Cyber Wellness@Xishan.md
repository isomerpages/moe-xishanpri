<h3>Cyber Wellness Week 2025</h3>
<p><strong>Wrapping Up Cyber Wellness Week!</strong>
</p>
<p>This year, we explored the theme <strong>"Pause, Think, Protect: Be Cyber Smart!"</strong>
</p>
<p>Our students had an insightful session with professionals from <strong>TOUCH Community</strong>,
where they learned about the <strong>S.U.R.E. framework</strong> during morning
assembly. A key takeaway? <strong>Not everything online is real</strong>—so
let's stay sharp, resilient, and safe from cyber threats.</p>
<p>During recess activities, students put their detective skills to the test
by analyzing whether news was real or fake based on given sources. Think
you can spot fake news?</p>
<p>To explore further, students can log into <strong>SLS</strong> to complete
the <strong>S.U.R.E. package</strong> and continue their cyber wellness journey.</p>
<p>#CyberWellnessWeek #StayCyberSmart #PauseThinkProtect #SUREFramework</p>
<hr>
<h3>Collaboration with Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) </h3>
<p>We also had the privilege of collaborating with the Cyber Security Agency
of Singapore (CSA) for an engaging and educational event on cyber safety.</p>
<p>The highlight was a skit by PlayInc, titled “Better Cyber Safe Than Sorry”,
which brought to life the importance of staying safe online — from spotting
scams and creating strong passwords, to building good cyber habits and
staying alert in today’s digital world.</p>
<p>We also explored the CSA Be Cyber Safe Pop-up, which featured an interactive
vending machine and games that tested our cyber knowledge. Participants
gained valuable insights on:</p>
<p>• Enabling 2FA</p>
<p>• Recognising malware</p>
<p>• Spotting phishing scams</p>
<p>Let’s keep practicing what we’ve learned — stay cyber smart, stay cyber
safe.</p>
