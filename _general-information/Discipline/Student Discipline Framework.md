<h1>Student Discipline Framework</h1>
<p>At Xishan Primary School, we are committed to providing a safe, respectful,
and supportive learning environment for every student. Clear expectations
and consistent responses help students understand boundaries, take responsibility
for their actions, and make positive choices. We view discipline as an <strong>educative and developmental process.</strong> Our
focus is on guiding students towards self-discipline, sound judgement,
and good character. Disciplinary interventions are intended to reflect
the consequences of poor choices made. All interventions are applied fairly,
firmly, and appropriately, with the aim of helping students learn, reflect,
and grow.</p>
